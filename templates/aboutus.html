Why Green IT Matters

Creating a sustainable digital future through responsible technology practices

We live in a digital age where technology plays a crucial role in our daily lives by powering businesses, communication and innovation. But the environmental impact of computing is often overlooked. From energy consumption to electronic waste, the IT sector contributes significantly to carbon emissions and resource depletion.

Green IT is the practice of designing, manufacturing, using, and disposing of computers, servers and other tech products in an environmentally sustainable way. By adopting Green IT principles, we can reduce energy consumption, minimize electronic waste and lower our carbon footprint.
